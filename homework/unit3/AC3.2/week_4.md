# Unit 3, Week 4 Homework

## Unit End Project

### Objective

Complete your team project and be prepared to present it on Monday.

## Lessons Learned

Each member of the team must prepare a 2-5 minute lesson on at least one topic he or she
learned about during the project. You can, and probably should, choose as a team which
topics each of you will present. But you are individually responsible for the topic you 
cover. They will be graded.

Put these mini-lessons into a README.md file in the root of your repo. Note: the root
of your repo might be one level up from the root of your XCode project. This is a 
"markdown" file. Markdown is a very simplified formatting "language" that all of our
lessons are written in. In fact this is a markdown file. You really don't need more 
formatting than what is illustrated in the following example but you might want to 
consult https://github.com/adam-p/markdown-here/wiki/Markdown-Cheatsheet.
